APPOINTMENT SCHEDULING POLICY
==============================
City Health Medical Center
Document Version: 2024-02
Department: Patient Access & Scheduling

OVERVIEW
--------
This document outlines the appointment scheduling policies for all
outpatient departments at City Health Medical Center. These policies
ensure fair access to care and efficient use of clinical resources.

HOW TO SCHEDULE AN APPOINTMENT
-------------------------------
Patients may schedule appointments through the following channels:

1. ONLINE: Visit myhealthportal.cityhealthmc.org (available 24/7)
2. PHONE: Call (800) 555-CARE, Monday–Friday 7:00 AM – 7:00 PM,
          Saturday 8:00 AM – 2:00 PM
3. MOBILE APP: Download "CityHealth Patient" from App Store or Google Play
4. IN-PERSON: Visit the Patient Access desk at the main lobby

For NEW patients, please have the following ready:
- Full legal name and date of birth
- Insurance card and photo ID
- Referral (if required by your insurance)
- Primary care physician name

APPOINTMENT TYPES
-----------------
- ROUTINE: Standard wellness checks and non-urgent issues.
           Booking window: 2–4 weeks in advance.
- URGENT: Conditions requiring care within 1–3 days.
          Available via phone only. Subject to provider availability.
- FOLLOW-UP: Post-treatment or post-hospitalization visits.
             Priority scheduling within 7–14 days of discharge.
- TELEHEALTH: Virtual consultations. See Telehealth Policy document.

CANCELLATION & RESCHEDULING POLICY
------------------------------------
- Cancellations must be made at least 24 HOURS before the appointment.
- To cancel or reschedule, call (800) 555-CARE or use the patient portal.
- Cancellations made less than 24 hours in advance may incur a
  no-show fee of $25 (waived for documented emergencies).
- After 2 consecutive no-shows, the patient may be required to call
  directly to schedule future appointments (online booking suspended).
- Providers reserve the right to reschedule appointments due to
  emergencies or unforeseen circumstances.

WALK-IN POLICY
--------------
Walk-in visits are accepted ONLY at the Urgent Care Center, located
at the East Wing entrance. Walk-ins are NOT accepted at specialty
departments. Same-day appointments for specialty departments may be
available by calling the scheduling line early in the morning.

APPOINTMENT REMINDERS
---------------------
- Automated reminder calls and SMS are sent 48 hours before appointments.
- Email reminders are sent 72 hours in advance (if email on file).
- Patients can confirm or cancel appointments directly through the reminder.

ARRIVING FOR YOUR APPOINTMENT
------------------------------
- Arrive 15 minutes early for your first visit (new patient paperwork).
- Bring a valid photo ID, insurance card, and a list of current medications.
- Appointments missed by more than 15 minutes may be rescheduled at the
  discretion of the department.

SPECIAL ACCOMMODATIONS
-----------------------
Patients requiring special accommodations (wheelchair, interpreter,
hearing assistance) should notify scheduling at the time of booking.
We offer free language interpretation services for all appointments.
